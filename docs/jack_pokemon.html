Jack's Pokemon

Live eBay listings filtered to raw (ungraded) Pokemon cards that fit Jack's collecting tastes. Each card is checked against the typical raw price for its category so you can tell at a glance whether it is a deal, fair, or overpriced.

How to read this page

Every listing shows what is being asked versus what the card typically sells for raw. If the ask is meaningfully below the typical raw price, it is a DEAL. Within plus-or-minus 20 percent it is FAIR. Twenty percent or more above is OVERPRICED — or TRAP if the title hints at condition risk. Cards marked Jack ★ fit his sweet spot: full-art / illustration rare or Japanese, and always raw.

  • Hard filter: no PSA, BGS, CGC, SGC, or slabs. Raw only.
  • Five-dollar minimum. No lots, commons, energies, or damaged copies.
  • Typical raw price is the median across raw comps in this scan.
  • TCGplayer market is pulled from PokemonTCG.io when there is a match.
