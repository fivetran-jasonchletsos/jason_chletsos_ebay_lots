How to use these two pages

  • Each guide covers the same five things in the same order: the goal, what's in a deck, how a turn works, the terms you'll actually hear, and a checklist for your first real game.
  • Both end with a section on building a starter deck out of whatever you've already pulled and scanned — no need to buy anything to try your first game.
  • Neither guide covers every rule. They cover enough to play a fun, legal-ish kitchen-table game. Look up edge cases as they come up — they will, and that's normal.
