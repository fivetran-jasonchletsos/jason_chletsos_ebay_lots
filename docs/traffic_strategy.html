Tier 2 — SEO unforced errors (this weekend, ~2 hours)

2
Fix the structured data gap and own the domain
Single biggest leverage
Add schema.org JSON-LD across the site
Verified by the SEO agent: index.html and by_set.html have zero schema.org markup despite Google Search Console being set up + sitemap + robots.txt valid. Add Product JSON-LD to every docs/items/*.html (name, image, sku, offers.price, brand) and Store JSON-LD to index.html with sameAs pointing to the eBay store. Unlocks Google rich-result eligibility.
Register harpua2001.com and CNAME to GitHub Pages $10-15/year · ~15 min setup
Brand-match domain ranks for branded queries immediately. GitHub Pages serves it free with HTTPS. Single CNAME file in docs/ + DNS A record at the registrar. Owned brand surface for the next decade.
Internal linking + breadcrumb JSON-LD
Every items/*.html should link back to its by_set and by_player parent. PageRank flows up. Add Breadcrumb JSON-LD to set + player pages for richer SERP appearance.

Build the email list with zero social

Your existing email_campaign_agent.py can fire a weekly "Steals" email today — there's just no list yet. Two capture mechanisms ranked:

  1. On-site "Steals Alerts" signup. Slim dismissable footer bar + single modal on index.html, deals.html, and item pages. "Get the weekly Steals list. New under-$10 finds, real photos, no fluff." Store in Buttondown (free under 100 subs). One afternoon of work.
  2. Post-purchase capture via Order Watch. When orders_watch_plan.json shows a new sale, auto-send a branded thank-you with a P.S. opt-in. eBay buyers who paid are the warmest list you'll ever own.
