What's New · Overnight, July 21–22

Here's what happened while you were asleep.

New pages, a couple hundred cards posted, one nasty bug squashed across every site, and a design glow-up for two of the sites that needed it.

New on the sites

  • The Ten Card Commandments — an original parody, playbook-style, now linked from the family hub.
  • Catch 'em all Claire — a brand-new Pokémon site and scanner, cloned from Natasha's Pokédex.
  • Learn to Play — beginner's guides for Pokémon TCG and Magic: The Gathering.
  • Suggested Lots pages added to The Sewer, Natasha's Pokédex, and the Eagles Nest — auto-grouped, ready-to-pull bundles.
  • The Sewer's nav got a cleanup: dropped a stray dead link, added Music and Pokémon boards, and unified the nav across every page.
  • An honest pricing explainer went up on The Sewer after Amanda flagged a real-world pricing gap on her Charlie Watts Prizm cards.
  • The Vault and Lola's Lot got a full design pass — real typography (Fraunces / Bagel Fat One), atmospheric backgrounds, and a staggered card-load animation, bringing them in line with the rest of the family.
  • A follow-up code review on that design pass caught and fixed 11 real bugs — a hover-feedback bug that silently broke on every card, background layers rendering in front of content instead of behind it, an entrance animation that was replaying on every keystroke, and a few font-weight/italic mismatches.

Basketball

  • Re-verified all 15 basketball scans by hand — corrected a false duplicate-scan flag and fixed undercounts on Isaiah Thomas, Nembhard, Ighodaro, and Peyton Watson.
  • 22 lots/singles posted live — the Spurs–Heat batch, 67 cards.
  • 7 more lots posted live — the Nuggets–Rockets batch, 25 cards.
  • Team and duo-insert cards (Celtics, Bulls, Wizards, Trail Blazers, Grizzlies, Mavericks, Raptors, Hawks) were pulled out of the sell lots per your instruction — 291 cards remain, team cards to be decided separately.
  • The basketball keeper PDF was re-sorted alphabetically by last name to make physical pulling easier.

Bug fixes

  • Fixed a card-vanishing bug across all 6 sites (The Vault, Lola's Lot, Natasha's and Claire's Pokédex, The Sewer, Eagles Nest) — scanning cards rapid-fire could silently drop one when two saves landed at the same time. This is the one that was quietly affecting Amanda's Sewer scans too.

Still in progress — needs your eyes

A pull-list valuation and a relics pricing PDF were generated overnight and are sitting in Downloads, waiting on a look.
