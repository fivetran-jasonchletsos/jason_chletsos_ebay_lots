Keep / Donate / Trash — 2-Second Rules

KEEP — Touched in last 2 months, active collection (Pokemon, Lego), obvious sentimental value, functional and used.
DONATE — You do not know what it is, younger-kid toy, duplicate, missing pieces, still deciding after 2 seconds.
TRASH — Wrappers, random paper, broken with no fix, mystery object with no clear owner.
