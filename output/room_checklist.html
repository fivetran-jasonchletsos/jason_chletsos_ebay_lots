1
Floor sweep
30 min
  • Every piece of clothing on the floor — laundry pile, no smell-testing
  • Wrappers, random paper, obvious trash — trash bag
  • Loose Lego pieces — corral into one bowl, set on the dresser. Do not sort tonight.
  • Anything with eBay potential (figures, games, collectibles in good shape) — dining room table
  • Guitar case — lean against the wall, out of the traffic lane
2
Upper bunk strip
20 min
  • Strip everything off the mattress
  • Tent and outdoor gear bag — out of the bedroom entirely (garage/basement)
  • Sleeping bags — keep 2, the third donates or goes to another closet
  • Extra blankets — keep 2 total, rest go to the hall linen closet
  • Extra pillows — keep 2 total, rest out
  • Nate's stuffies — small group at the pillow end of the upper bunk, 1 blanket laid out. Done.
3
Lower bunk + make both beds
20 min
  • All clothes off the lower bunk — laundry pile
  • Backpacks — 2 daily-use ones hang near the door or on closet hooks. Rest into the closet.
  • Strip and remake the lower bunk — flat, one pillow at the head, blanket. Hotel-style.
  • Tidy the upper bunk the same way.
4
Closet — gut and reload
50 min
  • Reposition the bunk ladder so it sits flush against the bunk frame, not blocking the closet door
  • Pull everything out of the closet
  • Books from the closet top shelf — move to the bookcase
  • Run the 2-second rule on everything that came out
  • Top shelf (hardest reach): tent bag, sleeping bags rolled, extra blankets folded flat
  • Middle shelves: bags and backpacks not in daily use, standing upright. Board games if any.
  • Lower shelves: one per boy for personal overflow
  • Closet floor: completely clear. Shoes in a row if anything.
  • Hooks or back of door: 2 daily-use backpacks hang here
5
Gaming desk + cable management
55 min
  • Clothes off the chair first — laundry pile
  • Sweep everything off the desk surface. Clean slate, then only put back what belongs.
  • Papers — school stuff into backpack, everything else trash
  • Guitar book — goes to the bookcase
  • Bundle all cables with zip ties. Power strip at the back-right of the desk. All cables run behind the monitor and down one desk leg. No cables crossing the front work surface.
  • PS5 stands vertically, white faceplate facing the door. Give it 8-10 inches of clear space around it.
  • Back on the desk: monitor, PS5, controller in its one designated spot, red lantern back corner. Nothing else.
  • Mini fridge — move to the floor on the left side of the desk
  • Lego builds — windowsill behind the desk or onto the bookcase. Off the desk surface.
6
Bookcase sort
30 min
  • Left column with the books — just straighten them upright. Leave it.
  • All other shelves — pull everything off, sort, reload with intention
  • Books from the closet — add them here
  • Bags and junk that was on the shelves — donate or eBay pile
  • One shelf: Lego boxed sets and instruction booklets. One shelf: card overflow if needed. Rest: books or leave empty.
7
Pokemon credenza + under-bed drawer + final sweep
35 min
  • Credenza: binders upright in a row, tins stacked together, figures grouped, plush clustered one end
  • Board games (Connect Four etc.) — closet shelf or eBay pile if complete
  • Wipe the credenza surface
  • Under-bed drawer: dump it, 2-second rule on everything, reload by section
  • Final walk — anything still on the floor finds a home
  • All trash bags and donate boxes go outside the bedroom door tonight
  • Turn on the red LED lantern on the desk
2-second rule: still deciding after 2 seconds = it donates. eBay pile = only if you immediately think "that sells." Keep moving.
